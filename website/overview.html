Crime in Metropolis

A data-story of crime in New York City

New York City is the largest city in America, with over 8.5 million inhabitants. With tales of gangsters ruling the mid-century streets and a rich history of, it's natural to expect that this massive city would come with massive amounts of crime.

Fortunately, detailed crime data has been collected by the city of New York since 2006, giving us the chance to peer into the nature of the city's crime.
